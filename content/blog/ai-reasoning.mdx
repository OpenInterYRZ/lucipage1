## Beyond Search

Remembering is powerful, but **reasoning** is transformative. LUCI's new reasoning engine doesn't just find information — it connects ideas across your entire history.

## Multi-Hop Reasoning

Ask LUCI a question like "What budget concerns were raised in meetings about Project X, and how do they relate to the Q3 planning document?"

LUCI will:

1. Find all meetings about Project X
2. Extract budget-related discussions
3. Locate the Q3 planning document
4. Synthesize connections between them

### Chain of Thought

LUCI shows its reasoning process, so you can verify each step and understand how it reached its conclusions.

## Proactive Insights

The reasoning engine also works in the background, surfacing connections you might have missed:

- Contradictions between meeting decisions and documentation
- Recurring themes across different projects
- Dependencies that span teams

## Limitations

We're transparent about what reasoning can and can't do. LUCI always shows confidence levels and cites its sources.

## The Road Ahead

This is just the beginning. We're working on collaborative reasoning, where LUCI can synthesize insights across an entire team's knowledge base.
