## What is LUCI?

LUCI is a personal AI assistant that goes beyond simple recording. It builds a **persistent memory** of everything you do on your computer — meetings, research, workflows — and uses that context to automate tasks intelligently.

Unlike traditional tools that require manual input, LUCI works in the background, continuously learning your patterns and preferences.

## How It Works

LUCI's core loop is simple:

1. **See** — LUCI observes your screen activity in real-time
2. **Remember** — Important context is stored in a structured knowledge graph
3. **Act** — When you need something done, LUCI has the full context to execute

### Persistent Memory

Every meeting, every document, every conversation — LUCI remembers it all. Need to recall what was discussed in last Tuesday's standup? Just ask.

### Smart Automation

LUCI doesn't just remember — it acts. Set up automated workflows that trigger based on context, not just schedules.

## Getting Started

Getting started with LUCI takes less than 2 minutes:

1. Download the LUCI desktop app
2. Sign in with your account
3. LUCI starts learning immediately

No complex setup. No configuration files. Just install and go.
