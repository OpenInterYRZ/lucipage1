## Our Privacy Philosophy

At LUCI, we believe that AI assistants should enhance your privacy, not compromise it. Every architectural decision we make starts with this principle.

## Local-First Processing

All screen analysis, entity extraction, and memory indexing happens on your device. We never send your screen content to external servers.

### End-to-End Encryption

When you choose to sync across devices, all data is encrypted end-to-end using keys that only you control.

### Zero-Knowledge Architecture

Our servers never see your unencrypted data. Even if our infrastructure were compromised, your information remains protected.

## Data Control

You have complete control over your data:

- **View** everything LUCI remembers
- **Delete** any memory at any time
- **Export** your data in standard formats
- **Pause** observation whenever you want

## Compliance

LUCI is designed to meet the requirements of GDPR, CCPA, and SOC 2. We undergo regular third-party security audits.

## Open Source Commitment

Our encryption and privacy modules are open source, so anyone can verify our claims.
