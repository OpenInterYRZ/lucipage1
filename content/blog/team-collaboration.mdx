## The Problem with Team Knowledge

In distributed teams, critical context gets lost in Slack threads, buried in documents, and forgotten after meetings. LUCI's Team Memory solves this.

## How Team Memory Works

Team Memory creates a **shared knowledge layer** that every team member can query. When someone makes a decision, discovers important context, or completes a milestone, it becomes part of the team's collective memory.

### Automatic Context Sharing

LUCI identifies shareable context from your individual workflows and — with your permission — surfaces it to team members who need it.

### Smart Notifications

Instead of noisy alerts, LUCI proactively provides relevant context when a team member is working on a related task.

## Use Cases

- **Onboarding** — new team members can query the team's history
- **Cross-functional projects** — design, engineering, and product share context seamlessly
- **Decision tracking** — never lose track of why a decision was made

## Privacy Controls

You always control what gets shared. Set boundaries per project, per topic, or per team.

## Getting Started

Team Memory is available now for teams on the Pro plan. Enable it from your team settings.
