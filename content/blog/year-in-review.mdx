## What a Year

Twelve months ago, LUCI was an idea. Today, it's used by thousands of people and teams worldwide. Here's how we got here.

## By the Numbers

- **50,000+** active users
- **10 million+** memories created
- **200+** integrations built by the community
- **99.9%** uptime

## Key Milestones

### Q1: Launch

We launched LUCI publicly in January and were overwhelmed by the response. The first month alone brought 5,000 signups.

### Q2: Team Memory

Our most requested feature shipped in May, enabling teams to share context while maintaining privacy.

### Q3: Reasoning Engine

Moving beyond recall to reasoning was our biggest technical challenge — and our biggest breakthrough.

### Q4: Enterprise

Launching LUCI Enterprise opened the door to larger organizations and more complex use cases.

## What We Learned

- **Privacy is non-negotiable** — users trust us with their most sensitive workflows
- **Speed matters** — every millisecond of latency in memory retrieval affects adoption
- **Simplicity wins** — the features people love most are the ones that just work

## Looking Ahead to 2026

We're just getting started. In 2026, expect:

- **LUCI Mobile** — your AI memory on the go
- **Advanced reasoning** — multi-agent collaboration
- **Industry-specific solutions** — tailored experiences for healthcare, legal, and finance

Thank you for being part of this journey.
